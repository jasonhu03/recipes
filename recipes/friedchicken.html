Fried Chicken Tenders

Description

This recipe is for yummy scrumptious fried chicken tenders

Ingredients

  • Chicken tenderloins
  • Peanut Oil
  • Flour
  • Eggs
  • Water
  • Desired Seasonings

Steps

  1. Place flour in bowl and mix with desired seasonings
  2. Mix eggs and water in second bowl
  3. Dip tenderloins in the egg mixture before coating with flour
  4. Not required but I recommend a second egg/flour coating
  5. Set aside battered chicken
  6. Heat oil to 375 degrees F
  7. Place battered chicken in oil and cook for 6-8 minutes or until golden brown
  8. Remove chicken and drain on paper towels before serving
  9. Enjoy!
